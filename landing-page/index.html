Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how li'l Mappo works.

Yes! The core studio is 100% free and open-source. Guests can export up to 720p/30fps with a 30-second limit. If you use the BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) feature, you instantly unlock unlimited 4K/60fps exports without paying us a dime.
Map providers charge for map loads. Instead of forcing you into a monthly subscription to cover our API costs, you can create a free Mapbox account, get your own public token, and paste it into li'l Mappo. This routes the map loads through your own generous free tier, allowing us to remove all export restrictions.
Absolutely. Whether you are on the Free tier, using BYOK, or on a paid subscription, the videos you generate are yours to use for YouTube channels, client work, broadcasting, or social media. No hidden licensing traps.
li'l Mappo leverages modern browser APIs like WebCodecs to render high-fidelity 60fps video directly on your local GPU. This requires the processing power and memory of a desktop or laptop environment. We prioritize professional-grade output over a quick mobile toy.
By default, all projects are saved locally in your browser's IndexedDB storage. You can also export `.lilmap` project files to your hard drive. If you create a free account, you unlock 3 Cloud Save slots. Subscribers get unlimited cloud auto-syncing across devices.
