Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains what information li'l Mappo collects, why, and what we do with it. We keep it plain. If something isn't clear, email us at support@lilmappo.tech.

What we collect

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address (required for sign-up).
  • If you sign in via Google or GitHub OAuth, your name and profile picture as provided by those services.
  • Your map load usage count (tracked server-side against your account).
  • Your projects, if you choose to save them to the cloud.

If you subscribe or make a payment, payment is handled by Dodo Payments. We do not store your card details. We receive a webhook notification confirming payment and record your subscription status in our database.

If you use Bring Your Own Key (BYOK), your Mapbox token is stored only in your browser's localStorage. It is never sent to our servers.

How we use it

  • To let you sign in and access your account.
  • To enforce free-tier usage limits.
  • To process your subscription and send you access to paid features.
  • To sync your saved projects across devices (if you're a subscriber).

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising.

Third-party services

li'l Mappo uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and cloud storage. supabase.com/privacy
  • Mapbox — Map rendering and routing. When you load the map, Mapbox receives standard request data (IP address, tile requests). mapbox.com/legal/privacy
  • Dodo Payments — Payment processing. dodopayments.com/privacy
  • Google / GitHub — Optional OAuth sign-in providers.
  • Vercel Analytics — Anonymous usage analytics on the hosted app. Collects page views, browser, OS, and country. No cookies, no personal data. vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

Data storage and retention

Account data is stored in Supabase on servers in England. We retain your account data for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, your data is deleted within 30 days.

Your rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Correct any inaccurate account information via your account settings.

To exercise any of these rights, email support@lilmappo.tech.

Cookies and local storage

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) for:

  • Storing your BYOK Mapbox token (never sent to our servers).
  • Remembering your pending checkout plan across OAuth redirects.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

Children

li'l Mappo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions? Email us at support@lilmappo.tech.
